About Prevail Painting

The principals of Prevail Painting have been in the painting business contracting with residential homeown- ers in addition to commercial companies for over Thirty years.

Prevail Painting specializes in performing work for residential homeowners and commercial companies throughout the Puget Sound Region. Prevail Painting is one of the areas most professional and reliable con- tractors. Prevail Painting is a family owned and operated business servicein Greater Columbus and surrounding areas.

Prevail Painting has night and weekend crews available for projects big and small. Our crews consist of highly motivated professionals that strive for excellence with each individual project. Prevail Painting is fully insured, providing all of our laborers with workers compensation and employer liability insurance. We provide professional service, reliability and satisfaction guaranteed.
